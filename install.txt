I. 	Copy over the catalog folder (yours may or may not use the catalog folder), namely these 2 files:

	1. catalog/includes/modules/header_tags/ht_price_display.php
	2. catalog/includes/languages/english/modules/header_tags/ht_price_display.php

II. 	In admin/modules.php, add this line... 

		require(DIR_FS_CATALOG . DIR_WS_CLASSES . 'currencies.php');

    	immediately after this line on the top of the file.
    
  		require('includes/application_top.php');

III. 	Go to Modules->Header Tags to install and active the price display for members only module.
